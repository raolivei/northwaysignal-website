# Northway Signal LLC

## Engineering Services for Scalable, Reliable Systems

Northway Signal LLC is a U.S.-registered engineering services company providing specialized support in cloud infrastructure, platform reliability, and large-scale systems operations.

We work with technology organizations that require dependable execution, clear accountability, and minimal operational friction.

---

## Services

Northway Signal provides engineering services across areas such as:

* Cloud infrastructure and platform engineering
* Reliability, observability, and monitoring systems
* Deployment automation and systems tooling
* Infrastructure modernization and migration initiatives
* Operational stability and performance optimization

Services are delivered under clearly defined scopes and integrate with existing client engineering teams and workflows.

---

## Engagement Model

Northway Signal operates as an independent services vendor.

* Direct services-for-consideration relationship
* Defined statements of work and deliverables
* No employment, staffing, or labor relationship
* No operational or managerial control over client teams

Clients contract directly with Northway Signal LLC, maintaining a clean and straightforward U.S. vendor relationship.

---

## Compliance & Structure

All contractual, tax, and compliance responsibilities are handled by Northway Signal LLC.

* U.S.-based contracting entity
* Standard enterprise documentation (W-9, NDAs, MSAs, SOWs)
* Invoicing in USD
* Clear separation between client operations and service delivery

This structure is designed to reduce legal, tax, and operational complexity for clients.
